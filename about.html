About BonziBuddy

BonziBuddy was a virtual assistant program introduced in the early 2000s. Known for its friendly purple gorilla, BonziBuddy could tell jokes, sing songs, and help users browse the internet.

While it became infamous over time for its… *unique* charm, it remains a nostalgic icon for many computer enthusiasts today.

On this site, we celebrate BonziBuddy in all its purple glory. Whether you’re here to relive the past or simply explore a quirky part of internet history, you’re in the right place.
