About BonziBuddy

BonziBuddy was a friendly purple gorilla desktop companion that could tell jokes, help you browse the web, and even sing songs. While it has become an internet legend, BonziBuddy remains a nostalgic piece of early 2000s software history.

Our mission at BonziBuddy.com is to preserve the fun and quirkiness of this classic digital friend, bringing him back for modern audiences to enjoy.
